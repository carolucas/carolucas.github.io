About Me

I'm currently a digital marketing student at the Startup Institute Chicago , a two-month professional accelerator for learning the hard and soft skills necessary to make a big impact working for a startup. Diving into html/CSS, Google Analytics and Adwords, community management, plus SEO and funnel optimization, I'm a full-stack marketer with a knack for understanding consumer behavior and a passion to leverage online tools to connect consumers with the products and services they need.

Previously, I served as a Community Business Development Volunteer in the Peace Corps in Armenia. For the past two years, I helped develop a startup women's artisan cooperative which created high quality handcrafted goods and employed economically disadvantaged women in the Berd region of Armenia. I helped design and develop an e-commerce website, created and managed social media accounts, and initiated strategic business development relationships with both nonprofit and for-profit partners.

Currently on the mission to align my passion (empowering women through with economic opportunity) along with a profession (digital marketing for a startup solving the world's pressing problems) here in Chicago. Feel free to connect with me at the links below!

Projects

GirlsLEAD Armenia Camp

Empowering girls through a summer camp promoting leadership, gender equality, volunteerism, creativity and community in the Berd region of Armenia. Funded by generous donors in our 2013 Indiegogo Campaign, you can keep up with the girls' journey at the Berd Women's Resource Center.

An project with the Homeland Development Initiative Foundation to creating joy through the production of high quality, handmade teddy bears by women artisans in rural Armenia. Originally funded by a Kickstarter Campaign in 2012, you can now purchase your own Berd Bear through our partner company, shopLAVENK.
